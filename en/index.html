More information
E-MOTION Fitness Community - is a new fitness club in the center of Kyiv. This is the space filled with a philosophy of healthy lifestyle, sports and emotional pleasure.

E-MOTION FITNESS COMMUNITY offers:
  • Classical fitness: group and individual trainings.
  • Fitness programs: 28 days of training focused to different directions
  • Outdoor programs: for people who like outdoor trainings.
  • Triathlon: Training that will help to prepare your body for a variety of physical activities and competitions.

You can achieve exactly your sporting goal in our club: lose weight, build muscles, recover after injury or prepare for sports competitions. Our qualified instructors are active athletes, national multiple champions, European winners, World Champions. They have both higher education and work experience in the field of fitness, rehabilitation and professional sports.
